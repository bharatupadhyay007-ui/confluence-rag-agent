# 📋 Bharat Upadhyay's Confluence RAG Agent

An AI agent that answers questions about banking remediation documents 
using RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) and local LLM inference.

No cloud. No API costs. Runs 100% on your machine.

---

## 💡 What it does

Ask questions in plain English about remediation readiness documents 
and get accurate, cited answers instantly.

**Example:**
- Input: `What is the total remediation amount for the loan fee waiver?`
- Output: Detailed answer with formula, figures, and source pages cited

---

## 🏦 Business Context

Banks and financial institutions accumulate years of remediation knowledge 
in Confluence — risk decisions, calculation methodologies, regulatory 
notifications, sign offs. Finding specific information across hundreds of 
pages takes hours.

This agent makes that knowledge instantly searchable in plain English — 
saving hours of manual searching and ensuring consistent, accurate answers.

---

## ✨ Features

- Plain English question answering over Confluence documents
- Automatic source citation — always know which page the answer came from
- Top 3 most relevant pages retrieved per question
- Question history
- Sample questions for quick exploration
- 100% local and private — no data leaves your machine
